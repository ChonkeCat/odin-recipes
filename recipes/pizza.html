Pizza

Description

This easy homemade pizza recipe gives you a crisp, golden crust topped with gooey melted cheese and flavorful sauce. With just a few simple ingredients, you can create a delicious pizza that rivals your favorite takeout spot. The beauty of this recipe is its flexibility — you can add your favorite toppings, from classic pepperoni to colorful veggies.

Perfect for a weekend dinner or a fun cooking night, this pizza is both satisfying and simple to make. Once you taste that freshly baked crust and bubbling cheese, you’ll never want to go back to frozen pizza again!

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.)

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough

    2. Preheat your oven to 220°C (425°F). Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface into your desired shape and thickness.

  2. Add sauce

    3. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.

  3. Add cheese and toppings

    4. Sprinkle mozzarella and parmesan over the sauce, then add your favorite toppings.

  4. Season and bake

    5. Drizzle olive oil around the edges, sprinkle with Italian seasoning and garlic, then bake for 12–15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.

  5. Slice and serve

    6. Remove from the oven, let cool for a few minutes, then slice and enjoy your homemade pizza!
