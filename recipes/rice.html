Fried Rice

Description

This simple fried rice recipe is quick, tasty, and perfect for using up leftover rice. Each grain is coated in flavorful soy sauce and stir-fried with vegetables, egg, and a touch of garlic for that classic takeout-style taste. It’s the perfect balance of savory, slightly nutty, and aromatic flavors that come together in just a few minutes.

Ideal as a side dish or a main meal, this fried rice can be customized with your favorite proteins or veggies. Whether you’re cooking for a busy weeknight or just craving something comforting, this dish delivers satisfying flavor and simplicity in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions (optional for garnish)

Steps

  1. Prepare ingredients

    2. Finely chop onion and garlic. Beat the eggs in a small bowl and set aside.

  2. Cook the eggs

    3. In a large pan or wok, heat oil and scramble the eggs. Remove and set aside.

  3. Stir-fry vegetables

    4. Add more oil if needed, then sauté onion, garlic, and mixed vegetables until soft.

  4. Add rice and sauce

    5. Stir in the cooked rice and soy sauce. Mix well to evenly coat the rice.

  5. Combine and serve

    6. Return the eggs to the pan, mix everything together, and season with salt and pepper. Garnish with green onions and enjoy!
