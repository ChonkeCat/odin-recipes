Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is the perfect blend of rich flavors and comforting layers. Tender sheets of pasta are stacked with a hearty meat sauce made from ground beef, tomatoes, garlic, and Italian herbs, creating a savory foundation that fills your kitchen with a mouthwatering aroma. A creamy ricotta and mozzarella cheese mixture adds a velvety texture, while a sprinkle of Parmesan on top bakes to a golden, bubbly perfection. Every bite delivers the perfect balance of cheesy, saucy goodness that makes lasagna such a beloved family favorite.

Whether you’re cooking for a cozy weeknight dinner or a special gathering, this lasagna is sure to impress. It’s easy to prepare ahead of time and tastes even better the next day as the flavors meld together. Serve it with a crisp green salad and garlic bread for the ultimate Italian comfort meal. This recipe is all about warmth, tradition, and the joy of sharing good food with the people you love.

Ingredients

  • ricotta cheese
  • spices
  • pasta sauce
  • ground beef
  • lasagna strips
  • onion
  • tomato paste
  • mozzarella
  • parmesan
  • egg
  • parsley
  • garlic

Steps

  1. Prepare meat sauce

    2. In a large pan, saute onions and add beef

  2. Mix cheese filling

    3. Combine ricotta cheese and egg

  3. Cook lasagna strips

    4. Boil lasagna strips according to package

  4. Assemble layers

    5. Spread meat evenly between the lasagna strips

  5. Bake and serve

    6. Bake at 190°C for 25 minutes, let rest for 10 minutes, then serve!
